### Before you start

1. **Clone** [VSCode_Readme](https://github.com/aadorian/VSCode_Readme) (or use your copy).
2. In VS Code: **File → Open Folder…** and select the **repository root** (the folder that contains `education/`).

Without that folder open, the **SE Tutorial:** commands will remind you to open the workspace—links open real files from the syllabus.

### Teaching tip

Assign one walkthrough step per lesson; students use **Command Palette** (`⇧⌘P` / `Ctrl+Shift+P`) and search **SE Tutorial:** to reopen any doc.
